## Bringing Saros to Life: Performance Capture Technology

The role of a **saros actor** in 2026 involves much more than standing in a recording booth. Housemarque utilized state-of-the-art performance capture (P-cap) technology to record Kohli's movements and facial expressions simultaneously. This ensures that the emotional weight of a scene is preserved, from the heavy breathing after a combat encounter to the subtle look of dread when the sun begins to die.

### The Mocap Process
1.  **Full-Body Tracking:** Kohli wore a specialized suit with markers to track every joint movement, ensuring Arun's "shoulder walk" and combat stances were authentic.
2.  **Facial Rigging:** High-resolution cameras were used to map Kohli's facial features, allowing the developers to translate complex emotions onto the character model of Arun Deage.
3.  **Voice Integration:** Unlike traditional dubbing, the voice lines were recorded during the physical performance to maintain natural cadence and physical effort sounds.
