## Deep Dive: Every Saros Deluxe Edition Armor Set

The centerpiece of the $80 bundle is undoubtedly the trio of inspired armor sets. These are not merely random designs; they are "fellow PS5 exclusive" inspired cosmetics that reflect the DNA of the developers' previous successes and Sony's broader portfolio.

### 1. The Astra Armor Set (Returnal Inspired)
Given that Saros is developed by the same creative minds behind the hit title *Returnal*, the Astra armor set is a direct nod to Selene’s iconic space suit. It features sleek, aerodynamic plating and a color palette that evokes the haunting, bioluminescent atmosphere of Atropos. For fans who resonated with the high-octane roguelike gameplay of the developers' previous work, this is often the most sought-after piece of **saros deluxe edition armor**.

### 2. The Onryo Armor Set (Ghost of Tsushima Inspired)
Taking inspiration from Sucker Punch’s *Ghost of Tsushima*, the Onryo set brings a samurai-inspired aesthetic to the world of Saros. "Onryo" refers to a vengeful spirit in Japanese folklore, and the armor reflects this with sharp angles and traditionalist elements blended into the game's futuristic setting. 

### 3. The Midgard Armor Set (God of War Inspired)
For those who prefer a more rugged, battle-hardened look, the Midgard armor set draws its lineage from the *God of War* series. Expect heavier plating and textures that suggest fur and leather, reminiscent of Kratos’ journey through the Nine Realms. It provides a visual weight that stands out significantly from the more slender Astra or Onryo sets.

### 4. Hands of Shore (Pre-order Bonus)
While not exclusive to the Deluxe Edition, the "Hands of Shore" set is included in the bundle if you purchase before the official launch date. This set is described as "cool but nothing crazy" by early reviewers, serving as a solid entry-level cosmetic for those who want to move away from the default starting gear immediately.
