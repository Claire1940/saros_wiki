## Performance Capture and Technical Excellence

The production of Saros utilized advanced "P-cap" (Performance Capture) techniques. Unlike traditional voice acting where lines are recorded in a booth, the **saros voice cast** spent months in specialized suits on a volume stage. This allowed the developers to capture every micro-expression and physical movement, ensuring that Arjun’s movements feel weighted and realistic.

The process involved several tiers of recording:
1.  **A-Class Scenes:** Full performance capture featuring body, face, and audio recorded simultaneously on a large stage.
2.  **B-Class Scenes:** Focused facial capture with actors in a booth, often used for dialogue-heavy sequences where physical movement is minimal.
3.  **C-Class Scenes:** Traditional voice-over work for background chatter and systemic barks.
