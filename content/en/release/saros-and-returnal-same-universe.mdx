## Mechanical DNA: From Atropos to the Hub World

One of the most significant departures from the Returnal formula is the introduction of a central hub. In Returnal, your only respite was the Helios crash site, which offered little in the way of permanent social interaction. Saros takes a page from the *Hades* playbook, providing a sanctuary where you can speak with NPCs, upgrade your gear, and witness story progression after every death.

This "come back stronger" motto defines the Saros experience. Unlike the nebulous progression of its predecessor, Saros makes your growth visible. You pay for permanent upgrades in the hub, though these are often gated behind boss encounters to prevent over-leveling. This ensures that the game remains a "proper rogue" while removing the frustration that many critics felt when they couldn't "roll credits" on Returnal due to its steep difficulty curve.

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Use your time in the central hub to exhaust all NPC dialogue. New story beats and potential questlines unlock specifically after you perish in the field, making death a vital tool for narrative discovery.

## Weapon Evolution: Archetypes vs. Perks

The gunplay in Saros offers a more nuanced system than the weapon drops in Returnal. While Returnal featured specific weapon types like the Tachyomatic Carbine or the Spitmaw Blaster, Saros utilizes "Archetypes" with latent perks that fundamentally change the weapon's identity.

For example, a standard rifle archetype can manifest as a **Tactical Rifle** if it possesses the "Rising Pitch" perk, or a **Smart Rifle** if it features "Split Smart Rounds." This means that finding a new weapon isn't just about a higher level; it's about how the inherent perks alter your combat strategy.

### Weapon Perk Scaling

This system allows for much deeper build customization. In Returnal, you were often at the mercy of RNG for specific traits. In Saros, the archetype system provides a more predictable yet diverse loot pool, rewarding players who learn the synergies between different latent abilities.
