## Combat Mechanics and Mastery

The gameplay is best described as "Returnal meets Doom Eternal." You are equipped with a zippy movement suite that includes dashing, shielding, and a high-risk parry. Mastering these mechanics is the fastest way to reduce **saros how long** your campaign lasts. 

The enemy projectile system uses a color-coded logic that players must memorize:
*   **Yellow Blasts:** Can be dashed through using R1.
*   **Blue Blasts:** Can be absorbed by the shield (L1) to charge a powerful counter-attack.
*   **Red Blasts:** Unblockable and undodgeable; these must be parried with precise timing.

## The Solar Eclipse: Risk vs. Reward

A unique mechanic that can either shorten or lengthen your playtime is the Solar Eclipse. At specific points in a level, you can trigger an eclipse to drastically increase the difficulty of the surrounding area. While this makes enemies more brutal, it also unlocks higher-tier loot rewards that are otherwise inaccessible.

When calculating **saros how long** a specific run should take, deciding whether to trigger an eclipse is a strategic gamble. If you are confident in your parry timing, the eclipse can grant you the firepower needed to melt a boss in minutes. If you fail, you may find yourself back at base camp, forced to regroup and spend your banked resources.

### Weapon Variety and Strategy

1.  **Auto Pistols:** Great for mobility and consistent chip damage.
