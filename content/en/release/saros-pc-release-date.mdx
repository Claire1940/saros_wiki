## Technical Challenges and Brand Management

Beyond the financial aspect, the technical overhead of PC porting has proven to be a headache for Sony's internal studios. Unlike the fixed hardware of the PS5 Pro, the PC market consists of thousands of different hardware configurations. 

1. **Optimization Issues:** Launching a game on PC that runs poorly can lead to "Review Bombing," which damages the game's prestigious reputation.
2. **The "Modding" Factor:** Sony is notoriously protective of its IP. The ability for users to mod characters or alter the cinematic vision of a game like *Saros* is often seen as a negative by the original creators.
3. **Visual Integrity:** Sony wants their games to be the "gold standard" for graphics. When a game is played on low settings on an aging PC, it doesn't represent the brand's high-fidelity goals.

Follow these steps if you still hope to play the game on your rig: monitor official [PlayStation Blog](https://blog.playstation.com/) updates, as any change in strategy would be announced there first. Avoid relying on "leaked" release windows from retail sites, as these are often placeholders used to drive traffic.
