## Gameplay Mechanics: Beyond Returnal

While *Returnal* focused on a roguelike loop, *Saros* shifts the focus toward a structured single-player narrative while maintaining the fluid movement and challenging combat Housemarque is known for. The **rahul kohli saros** experience centers on Arun Deage’s abilities as an enforcer, utilizing high-tech weaponry and supernatural powers to survive a planet that is literally trying to consume him.

### The Shape-Shifting World
The planet in *Saros* is described as "hostile and shape-shifting." Unlike the procedural generation of Atropos in *Returnal*, the world of *Saros* undergoes massive transformations triggered by an "ominous eclipse." These environmental shifts change the layout of the map in real-time, forcing players to adapt their strategies mid-combat.

> **💡 Tip:** Pay close attention to the skybox. The progression of the eclipse often signals an imminent change in enemy behavior and terrain layout.

### Combat and Progression
As Arun Deage, players will have access to an arsenal that reflects his status as a powerful enforcer. The combat loop emphasizes:
1. **Verticality:** Using jet-assisted dashes and grappling hooks to navigate crumbling environments.
2. **Eclipse Buffs:** Harnessing the energy of the eclipse to power up specific abilities.
3. **Tactical Dismemberment:** Targeting specific weak points on massive, shape-shifting entities.
