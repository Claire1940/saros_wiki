## Combat and the Cycle of Death

One of the most praised aspects of the **saros release** is its "Come Back Stronger" mechanic. Much like traditional roguelikes, falling in battle returns you to the shore, but you retain "Answers"—a form of currency used to unlock permanent stat boosts and new abilities. This ensures that every attempt, no matter how short, contributes to your overall power.

The combat focuses on fluidity and timing. Players must manage their "Light Gauge," which depletes when venturing into shadowed areas. If the gauge reaches zero, your health begins to drain rapidly, forcing a more aggressive playstyle to find light sources or defeat "Luminous" enemies.

| Combat Action | Effect | Best Used When... |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Light Burst** | **Stuns nearby shadows** | You are surrounded by more than three enemies. |
| **Eclipse Parry** | **Reflects dark energy** | A boss is charging their primary beam attack. |
| **Sun Dash** | **Brief invulnerability** | Navigating through lethal floor hazards. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not hoard your "Answers." If you die twice without spending them at the Shoreline Shrine, you risk losing a portion of your accumulated power permanently.
