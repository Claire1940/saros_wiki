## Gameplay Evolution: The Hub and Meta-Progression

One of the biggest departures in Saros is the introduction of a central hub. Unlike the lonely crash site in *Returnal*, Saros features a bustling base of operations where you can interact with NPCs. This "Hades-style" approach allows for more direct storytelling and permanent upgrades that are clearly visible to the player.

### The Central Hub Mechanics

The hub serves as the heart of the game’s meta-progression. Here, you can spend currency earned during runs to unlock permanent stat boosts.

1.  **NPC Dialogues:** After every death, NPCs in the hub provide new lore snippets and side quests.
2.  **Stat Upgrades:** Invest in health, damage, and perk frequency.
3.  **Boss Gating:** Certain upgrades are locked until you defeat specific world bosses, preventing players from becoming "over-leveled" too early.
4.  **Weapon Training:** Test out new archetypes in a safe environment before heading into the biomes.

## Weapon Archetypes and Combat Fluidity

Combat in Saros feels like a refined version of the *Returnal* system. While the previous game featured a variety of weapons, Saros introduces "Archetypes" that define how a gun behaves. Instead of just finding a "Carbine," you might find a "Tactical Rifle" or a "Smart Rifle," each with latent perks that fundamentally change the firing rhythm.

For example, a **Tactical Rifle** might feature "Rising Pitch," where the fire rate increases the longer you hold the trigger. Conversely, a **Smart Rifle** might utilize "Split Smart Rounds" that track multiple enemies simultaneously. This variety ensures that every run feels distinct, even if you favor a specific weapon class.

### Weapon Leveling and Perks

As you progress through the biomes, the level of the guns you find will increase. Higher-level weapons come with more perk slots, allowing for devastating combinations.

*   **Level 1-5:** Usually 1 basic perk (e.g., Super Load).
*   **Level 6-10:** 2 perks, often including a "Spectral Round" or utility boost.
*   **Level 11+:** 3+ perks, creating "God Tier" variants that can clear rooms in seconds.
