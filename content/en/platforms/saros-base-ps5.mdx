## Permanent Progression and Armor Attributes

The core philosophy of Saros is to "come back stronger." While your weapons and certain artifacts may be lost upon death, the attributes you invest in through the **saros base ps5** Primary system remain constant. This permanent progression focuses primarily on Arjun’s armor and his inherent tactical abilities.

When you interact with Primary, you spend resources gathered during your excursions to Carcosa to enhance your armor's attributes. These upgrades are essential because the hostiles on Carcosa grow exponentially more brutal as you delve deeper into the shapeshifting biomes.

### Key Armor Attributes to Prioritize

1.  **Integrity:** Increases your maximum health pool, allowing you to survive more hits from corrupted projectiles.
2.  **Weapon Proficiency:** Improves the base damage and handling of all found weaponry.
3.  **Perk Slots:** Unlocks additional spaces for special passive abilities that can change the flow of combat.
4.  **Energy Recovery:** Speeds up the recharge rate of your power weapons and special gadgets.

> **💡 Tip:** Early in the game, prioritize Armor Integrity and Perk Slots. Survival is more important than raw damage when you are still learning the attack patterns of Carcosan hostiles.

Saros requires a balance; every advantage you give yourself must be weighed against the consistency of the challenge.

| Modifier Type | Effect on World | Risk/Reward |

## The Eclipse: High-Risk Corruption Mechanics

As you explore Carcosa, you will eventually encounter alien devices that allow you to "call the Eclipse." This is a fundamental escalation of the **saros base ps5** gameplay loop. The Eclipse is a state of total corruption that alters the audio, visuals, and mechanical rules of the planet.

During an Eclipse, hostiles shoot corrupted projectiles. These are particularly dangerous because they don't just deal damage; they reduce your **maximum armor integrity**. If your max integrity drops too low, even the weakest enemies can end your run in a single strike. To counter this, you must use your Power Weapon. Dealing damage with Power Weapons is the only way to restore your maximum integrity during the Eclipse phase.

### Eclipse Corruption Effects

*   **Weapon Corruption:** Your firearms gain massive power boosts but may suffer from negative traits like increased recoil or slower reload speeds.
*   **Artifact Corruption:** Passive items become "Corrupted Artifacts," providing game-changing benefits at a significant cost (e.g., increased damage at the expense of health regeneration).
*   **Visual/Audio Distortion:** The world becomes more chaotic, requiring players to rely on their spatial awareness and haptic feedback on the PS5 DualSense controller.

## Teleportation and Biome Management

Efficiency is a hallmark of the **saros base ps5** design. Once you have unlocked a biome by reaching it at least once, you can use the teleportation system at Primary to skip straight to the action. This removes the "slog" often associated with the genre, allowing you to get back to the specific challenge that killed you previously.

Replayability is enhanced through this system. If you find yourself struggling in a later biome, you can quickly restart a selected earlier biome to farm for specific artifacts or weapon upgrades before moving forward. This strategic layer allows players to "build" their run before entering the most dangerous zones.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Teleporting directly to a later biome means you start with fewer artifacts than if you had played through the preceding areas. Only skip ahead if you are confident in your base attributes.
