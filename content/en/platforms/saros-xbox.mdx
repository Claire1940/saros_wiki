## Permanent Progression and Customization

Unlike traditional roguelikes where you lose everything upon death, Saros utilizes a "Permanent Progression" system managed through the Primary interface. This allows players to invest in their character's longevity and power over dozens of hours of gameplay.

1. **Armor Attributes:** Upgrade your suit's defense, speed, and energy capacity using Carcosan resources.
2. **Special Perks:** Unlock unique abilities that trigger under specific combat conditions, such as "Perfect Reload" or "Kinetic Burst."
3. **Carcosan Modifiers:** Adjust the game's difficulty by making hostiles tougher or weaker. You can even remove the "second chance" ability for a true hardcore experience.
4. **Teleportation:** Once a biome is unlocked, you can use Primary to teleport there directly, allowing you to skip earlier sections and jump straight into the action.

> **💡 Tip:** Always balance your modifiers. If you make enemies weaker, your resource gain will drop significantly, slowing down your permanent upgrades.
