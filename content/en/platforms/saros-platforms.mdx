## The "Comeback Stronger" Progression System

A core component of the gameplay across all **saros platforms** is the "Comeback Stronger" system. In Saros, death is not a failure but a mechanic for growth. When Arjun Devraj falls in combat, he returns to "The Passage," a hub area where survivors of Echelon 4 gather. Here, players interact with "Primary," the voice of Sultari, to engage with permanent progression systems.

Unlike traditional roguelikes where you might lose everything, Saros allows for meaningful upgrades to your armor's attributes and the acquisition of special perks that persist across runs. This system is designed to balance the inherent difficulty of the shape-shifting planet with a sense of constant forward momentum.

### Key Progression Elements:
*   **Armor Attributes:** Increase your base integrity and defense against corrupted projectiles.
*   **Special Perks:** Unlock unique abilities that trigger during the Eclipse or when using power weapons.
*   **Carosen Modifiers:** Adjust the challenge level by making hostiles tougher or weaker.
*   **Second Chance:** A toggleable ability that allows players one revival per run, though it can be removed for a greater challenge.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Removing the "Second Chance" ability via Carosen Modifiers will increase your score multiplier but makes death permanent for that specific run.

## The Eclipse: High-Stakes Gameplay and Visual Escalation

The most unique feature found on the official **saros platforms** is the Eclipse system. Triggered by an alien device, the Eclipse corrupts the world of Carcosa, leading to a massive escalation in difficulty. During an Eclipse, hostiles fire corrupted projectiles that do more than just damage; they reduce your maximum armor integrity.

To combat this, players must utilize "Power Weapons." Landing hits with these specialized tools can restore lost armor integrity, creating a high-risk, high-reward gameplay loop. Furthermore, the Eclipse corrupts weapons and artifacts found in the world. These corrupted items often provide massive power boosts but come with negative trade-offs, requiring players to carefully weigh the pros and cons of their loadout.
