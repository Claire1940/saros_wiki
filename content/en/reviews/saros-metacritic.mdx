## DualSense and 3D Audio: The Immersion Factor

A significant portion of the **saros metacritic** score will likely be influenced by the game’s use of the DualSense controller and 3D audio. Housemarque is known for using haptic feedback not just as a gimmick, but as a gameplay mechanic. In *Saros*, the adaptive triggers offer varying resistance based on the weapon's heat levels and firing modes.

3D audio plays an equally vital role. In a fast-paced third-person shooter, knowing exactly where a projectile is coming from can be the difference between a successful run and a game over. Critics have noted that the sound design in *Saros* is "essential" for situational awareness, particularly in the later, more difficult biomes.

1.  **Haptic Feedback:** Feel the texture of different planetary surfaces and the impact of enemy projectiles.
2.  **Adaptive Triggers:** Weapon functions change based on trigger pressure, allowing for secondary fire modes without extra buttons.
3.  **3D Audio:** Use [Sony's official 3D audio headsets](https://www.playstation.com/en-us/accessories/pulse-elite-wireless-headset/) for maximum spatial awareness.
4.  **SSD Loading:** Near-instant restarts ensure that the momentum of the roglike loop is never broken.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Playing without a 3D audio-capable headset may put you at a disadvantage during boss fights, as many audio cues for off-screen attacks are spatial.
