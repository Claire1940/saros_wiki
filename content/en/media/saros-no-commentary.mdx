## Navigating the Ancient Geo Network

To progress through the main campaign, you must interface with the Geo Network. This is a series of interconnected nodes linked to central hubs that control the planet's massive drilling machines and gate systems. Accessing these nodes is often a matter of high risk versus high profit analysis.

### Steps to Activate a Network Node:
1. **Locate the Hub:** Follow the Sultari signal through the underground tunnels.
2. **Clear the Area:** Hostile entities often enter a "Security Response" mode once a node is approached.
3. **Trigger the Console:** Use your integration modules to override the lock.
4. **Prepare for Lockdown:** Gates may lock behind you, requiring you to find an alternative route or a teleportation node.

## Survival Strategies for the Eclipse

The Eclipse is more than just a visual change; it is a gameplay-altering event. When the sun "sees you," as the survivors put it, the world becomes more hostile. You will hear the "whisper," and your HUD will likely flash warnings about extreme kinetic energy requirements. 

Watching **saros no commentary** playthroughs reveals that experienced players often use the Eclipse to farm enriched Lucenite, despite the increased danger. This resource is essential for high-level upgrades but comes at the cost of facing "unlocked entities" that have no recorded data in your scout's database.

> **Tip:** If you hear the "voice" during an eclipse, seek shelter in a tunnel immediately. The psychological "break" mechanic can cause your controls to become unresponsive or your map data to reset entirely.
