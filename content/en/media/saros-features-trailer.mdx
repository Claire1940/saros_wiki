### Key Locations within the Reef

## Character Progression and Customization

Progression in this universe is tied to the "Core" of your suit. As you explore and defeat "the things from the reef," you collect Solar Shards. These shards are used to upgrade your suit's capabilities across three distinct trees: Vanguard, Engineer, and Mystic.

The **saros features trailer** briefly shows a menu screen where players can swap "Mod Chips" on the fly. This suggests a highly modular approach to builds, allowing you to pivot your strategy based on the specific boss you are facing. For instance, if a boss is resistant to kinetic damage, you can quickly slot in Thermal or Electrical mods to gain the upper hand.

| Upgrade Path | Focus | Key Ability |

This mode will feature its own separate progression track and exclusive cosmetic rewards, ensuring longevity for the title well after the main credits roll.
