The centerpiece of the **saros deluxe edition** is undoubtedly the trio of armor sets inspired by fellow PlayStation 5 exclusives. These are not just random cosmetics; they are designed to evoke the spirit of the games they represent.

Given that the developers of Saros also created Returnal, the Astra set is a direct nod to Selene’s journey on Atropos. It features sleek, futuristic lines and a color palette that mirrors the high-tech, alien-fighting gear found in the acclaimed roguelike.

## 48-Hour Early Access: Is It Worth It?

For many hardcore fans, the most valuable part of the **saros deluxe edition** isn't the armor—it's the time. The 48-hour early access window allows players to jump into the world of Saros two full days before the general public.

In the modern gaming landscape, early access has become a polarizing but popular feature. For a game like Saros, which is expected to have a dedicated community following the success of the developer's previous titles, those 48 hours can be crucial for:
*   Avoiding social media spoilers.
*   Getting a head start on mastering game mechanics.
*   Climbing early-game leaderboards or community rankings.

However, if you are a casual player who doesn't mind waiting a few days, the $10 premium might feel steep just for a 48-hour window. It is important to weigh your personal "hype level" against the cost of the upgrade.
