## Exclusive Armor Sets and Inspirations

The centerpiece of the Digital Deluxe Edition is the trio of armor sets inspired by iconic PlayStation franchises. For players who enjoy cross-over content, these sets provide a unique aesthetic flair that pays homage to the history of the platform.

### Astra Armor Set (Returnal Inspired)
Given that the creators of Saros are the same minds behind the hit title *Returnal*, the Astra armor set is a direct nod to Selene’s journey on Atropos. It features sleek, high-tech plating and a color palette that mirrors the scout suits found in the roguelike shooter.

### Onryo Armor Set (Ghost of Tsushima Inspired)
For fans of Sucker Punch’s samurai epic, the Onryo set brings a touch of feudal Japan to the sci-fi world of Saros. This set typically features more organic textures and silhouettes that evoke the spirit of the Ghost, modified to fit the futuristic setting of the game.

### Midgard Armor Set (God of War Inspired)
The Midgard set is designed for players who want to feel like a powerhouse. Inspired by Kratos’ journey through Norse mythology, this armor features heavier plating and rugged details that suggest a warrior capable of taking down gods.
