## The Eclipse: Escalating the Challenge

One of the most innovative features of Saros is the "Eclipse" mechanic. By activating an alien device, players can call the Eclipse, which corrupts the world of Carcosa. This isn't just a visual change; it's a complete escalation of gameplay. Hostiles begin firing corrupted projectiles that reduce your maximum armor integrity. 

In a **saros multiplayer** environment, the Eclipse serves as a high-risk, high-reward event. While the challenge is ramped up, the potential for acquiring corrupted weapons and artifacts—which offer significant power boosts—is much higher.

### Managing Corruption Levels

1. **Utilize Power Weapons**: You can restore lost armor integrity by successfully landing hits with your power weapon.
2. **Balance Modifiers**: Use the "Carcosan Modifiers" at Primary to adjust hostile toughness.
3. **Teleport Strategically**: Use the biome teleport system to quickly restart a biome if a teammate falls early in a run.
4. **Artifact Synergy**: Corrupted artifacts alter your gameplay in both positive and negative ways. Communicate with your partner to ensure your "negative" modifiers don't negate their "positive" ones.

> **💡 Tip:** Always keep one player focused on "Integrity" restoration while the other focuses on "Eclipse" corruption management to maintain a steady frontline.
